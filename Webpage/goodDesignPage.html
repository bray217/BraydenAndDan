Good Web Design

What makes a good design? How can you have good design? Heuristics

A good webpage will try and follow Nielsen's heuristics, 10 ideas that sum up how users can interact with interfaces. This is the standard for good design across the board

By following Nielsen's heuristics (right) and having competent systems in place you can quite easily have good design. The main hueristics for good design are Aesthetic and Minimalist design and Consistency and Standards, these will make your webpage look nice, the other heuristics focus more on functionality.
